What is an apprenticeship

Apprenticeships can boost your business, giving you control over funding for apprenticeships and provide your staff with the practical skills needed to grow your business.

The Levy explained

In England control of apprenticeship funding is moving to employers. The apprenticeship levy is a tax on employers with a paybill of more than £3 million to help fund new apprenticeships. We've created an guide to the Levy to help you understand it.

Are you ready to start?

With thousands of businesses taking on apprenticeships, the competition is tough. Being prepared is key to taking advantage of the opportunities available to develop your existing staff and attract new talent into your business.

Order and info pack and one of our dedicated team will be on hand to help you. Don't forget to download and read all the resources we've created. They are there to help you every step of the way from understanding the new Apprenticeship Standards, to getting registered for funding and recruiting an apprentice.

What can an AAT Apprenticeship do for your business?

  • Boost your business

    Make your business more competitive and your employees more motivated.

  • Give you financial control

    The new levy puts financial control for apprenticeship training in your hands.

  • Improve recruitment

    Reduce your recruitment costs and increase employee retention.

  • Provide practical skills

    AAT trained and qualified staff learn techniques and process they can use to improve efficiency.
